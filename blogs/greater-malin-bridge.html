After accidentally getting the tram to Malin Bridge, and taking some pictures to augment my end-of-the-line entry, I decided to explore the area a little bit. Turns out, there was quite a lot to see – nature and post-industrial waste (what else is there in Sheffield)?

The first thing I found going out down the road is an abandoned factory. Of course I had to investigate.

I had a peek through the slats of the door to see what else I could discern about the site, and it clearly hadn't seen use in some time. I did have to ask, however, what exactly "EX" meant. My mind went to explosives, which is certainly a fun explanation.
